Read before uploading. These steps ensure your photos look perfect on the website.
1
Prepare your photo
Portrait orientation — taller than wide. Ideal: 900×1200px or larger. JPEG or PNG. Under 5MB. Use a clean background — white, cream, stone or dark.
Portrait JPEG • 900×1200px minimum
2
Tap “Add a Look” tab above
Fill in Brand, Look Name, Price and Season. Brand and Look Name are required. All other fields are optional.
3
Upload your photo
Tap Choose Photo and select from your camera roll or files. A preview appears immediately so you can confirm before saving.
Preview appears immediately after selecting
4
Feature on landing page
Tick “Show on landing page” for your 4 strongest looks. These appear in the preview grid at the top of the website. Leave unticked for the full collection only.
Maximum 4 featured looks on the landing page
5
Tap “Add to Lookbook”
The look appears on the website immediately. Go to Manage Looks to remove the placeholder looks one by one as you replace them with real photos.
Changes appear on the website instantly
Fill in the details, choose a photo, and tap Add. The look appears on the website immediately.
