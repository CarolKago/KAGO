Lookbook Management
Add, edit and remove looks from the website lookbook.
Read this before uploading. Following these steps ensures your photos look perfect on the website.
1
Prepare your photo
Portrait orientation — taller than wide. The website cards use a 3 to 4 ratio. Ideal size is 900 by 1200 pixels or larger. JPEG or PNG format. Keep the file under 5MB. A clean background — white, cream, stone or dark — photographs best on the cards.
Portrait JPEG • 900×1200px or larger
2
Go to Add a Look
Tap the Add a Look tab above. Fill in the Brand, Look Name, Piece Description, Price and Season. Brand and Look Name are required. All other fields are optional but improve the client experience.
3
Upload your photo
Tap Choose Photo and select your image from your camera roll or files. A preview appears immediately so you can confirm it looks right before saving. You can upload from your iPad camera roll directly.
A preview appears immediately after selecting
4
Feature on the landing page
Tick Show on landing page if you want this look in the 4-card preview at the top of the website. Only 4 featured looks appear there. Your strongest and most editorial looks should be featured. Leave unticked for looks that only appear in the full collection below.
Feature your 4 strongest looks on the landing page
5
Add to Lookbook
Press the button. The card appears on the website immediately. Go to Manage Looks to remove the placeholder looks and replace them one by one with your real photographs. The lookbook holds up to 10 looks total.
Changes appear on the website instantly
6
Important note about photos
Photos are saved in your browser storage. If you clear your browser or switch devices, you will need to re-upload them. For a permanent solution when you are ready, come back to Claude and we will embed the photos directly into the website files so they are always there.
Re-upload if you switch browsers or clear cache
