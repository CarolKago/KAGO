The Portfolio

Styling Concepts

I
The Executive
The boardroom woman who commands silence when she enters. Brunello Cucinelli ivory linen blazer, sharply structured. Loro Piana cashmere trousers. Manolo Blahnik nude patent heels. Bottega Veneta intrecciato clutch. Understated. Immovable.
II
The Femme Fatale
McQueen's sculpted blazer, nipped at the waist. A sheer black blouse beneath. Black stockings, Louboutin Pigalle. Saint Laurent Sac de Jour in black crocodile. This woman does not need to raise her voice. The room rearranges itself for her.
III
The Archive Collector
1960s Dior Bar jacket, ivory wool. Sourced from a Paris estate. Paired with contemporary Ralph Lauren pencil skirt. Chanel vintage 2.55 in lambskin. This is not fashion — this is inheritance.
IV
The Evening Architect
YSL Le Smoking tuxedo, midnight black. No jewellery. One statement: Cartier Tank watch. The Row satin clutch. Louboutin So Kate. This is the woman who arrives last and leaves first — and everyone remembers only her.
